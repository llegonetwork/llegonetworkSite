# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains what data, if any, is collected across websites, bots, and other services operated under the llegonetwork name. It's written as a single shared policy so it can be linked to from any llegonetwork site or project, rather than maintained separately for each one.

The short version: **very little is collected anywhere, and nothing beyond what's needed for a Service to function is ever sold, shared with advertisers, or used to build a profile of you.**

## 1. Websites

Websites under the llegonetwork name generally do **not**:
- Use tracking cookies
- Use third-party analytics (e.g. Google Analytics)
- Run advertising scripts or ad networks
- Collect IP addresses beyond what a hosting provider logs automatically as standard web traffic, which llegonetwork does not access or process

Any settings stored (such as theme or display preferences) are typically saved locally in your own browser and are never sent to us, unless a specific feature says otherwise.

## 2. Sign-In & Third-Party Accounts

Some Services may offer sign-in through a third-party provider, such as Discord OAuth, instead of a traditional account system.

- **Collected:** Only the basic account information the provider shares based on the sign-in permissions ("scopes") shown to you at the time — typically an account ID, username, and avatar. Passwords are never seen or stored by llegonetwork; authentication is handled entirely by the provider.
- **Purpose:** To recognize you across visits to a Service and associate any Service-specific data (like settings or activity) with your account.
- **Retention:** Kept only as long as your account remains linked to that Service. You can request this data be deleted at any time (see Section 6).

## 3. Bots

Bots inherently have access to certain information through the platform they run on (such as the Discord API) in order to function — this is standard for any bot on that platform, not something collected outside of the platform's normal operation. In general, across llegonetwork bots:

- **Collected:** Server/community IDs, channel IDs, and user IDs (platform account numbers, not real names or emails), plus whatever message content or interaction is needed at the moment a command or feature is used.
- **Purpose:** To execute commands, features, moderation actions, statistics, or AI-assisted responses as designed for that specific bot.
- **AI-assisted features:** Where a bot uses an AI provider to generate responses, your prompt (and a short rolling conversation history, where applicable) is sent to that provider to generate a reply. This is subject to that provider's own privacy policy in addition to this one. Conversation history used for context is temporary and is not sold, permanently archived, or used to build a profile of you.
- **Retention:** Any per-server or per-user configuration/statistics data (such as leveling progress or custom settings) persists only as long as the bot remains active in that server, or until deletion is requested.

## 4. Other Projects

Standalone projects (games, tools, demos, etc.) published under the llegonetwork name are generally client-side and collect no data at all beyond what's described above for websites, unless a specific project states otherwise.

## 5. What Is Never Collected

Across every llegonetwork Service, we never collect:
- Real names, physical addresses, or phone numbers
- Email addresses (unless you personally email us, or a sign-in provider shares one you've made public through your account)
- Payment or financial information
- IP addresses beyond standard hosting logs we don't access
- Device fingerprinting or cross-site tracking data
- Any data sold or shared with advertisers or data brokers

## 6. Data Sharing, Retention & Deletion

Data is **never sold**. It is only "shared" in the limited sense that a Service's core functionality requires it — for example, sending a prompt to an AI provider, or receiving basic profile info from a sign-in provider. We do not share data with advertisers, analytics companies, or any other unrelated third party.

You can request deletion of any data associated with your account or user ID on any llegonetwork Service at any time by contacting us. Removing a bot from your server, or disconnecting a linked sign-in account, will also stop further data collection for that Service going forward. If a Service is discontinued, any data collected while it was active remains subject to this Privacy Policy until deletion is requested.

## 7. Children's Privacy

llegonetwork Services are not directed at children under 13, and no data is knowingly collected from users known to be under that age, consistent with the minimum age requirements of platforms like Discord.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as projects evolve, since it's a single shared document covering every current and future llegonetwork Service. Continued use of any Service after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions, or requests to view/delete data associated with your account, can be sent to **[contact@llegonetwork.dev](mailto:contact@llegonetwork.dev)**.
